How we do it

Sublimation is the transition of a substance directly from a solid state to a gas state. It does not pass through the usual liquid state, and only occurs at specific temperatures and pressures.

Using this technique, we are able to print any design out using a specific sublimation ink, paper, printer, and heat press, so that the ink is transferred and bonds directly to the material of the shirt.

Here is the process you can expect at your next visit!

  • First, you pick a shirt from our rack with two colors available at the moment, sand and white.
  • After choosing your shirt, one of our team members will prepare it so that it can be sublimated on.
  • Then you can take some time to sit down and design something sweet on one of our tablets and enjoy the ambiance of the Tee House
  • Once your design is finished, one of our team members will size it to your liking and print it out.
  • Lastly, your design will be placed on the shirt underneath the heat press and voila, you have your very own customized shirt!

Make sure that before you leave you snag a picture of you and your shirt and tag us @teehouses on instagram!
